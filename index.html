Jean Hidalgo - Brand Motorcycle

This is great news!! Awesome I would love share my experience here are my answers to your questions.

What was the hardest part of your Motorcycle to fix?

The hardest part to fix was the tank it had several dents and a big one on the side, it was definitely a challenge because i wanted to keep the bike with most of the original parts.

How many vehicles have you rebuilt?

This was my first rebuild after that one i did a 2006 CBR1000 which i also got at copart.

What was your budget on the Rebuild?

My budget was set at $600.00 and i ended up spending 618.90

How long did it take from start to finish?

It took me 2 months working every weekend on it.

Why did you pick this motorcycle?

I chose a motorcycle because i always wanted to have a Harley and they are so expensive, because of copart was the only way i could've afford it.

Did you do it all yourself?

I did everything myself including body work and paint, also a few mechanical fixes.

Tell us a little about yourself.

I am a sales rep for an aviation company, im a automotive enthusiast, I also love motorcycles, my next project is a minivan for me and my family we do enjoy long trips on the road and i would like something confortable like a honda oddssey or a toyota sienna, its been really difficult to locate one at a reasonable price. Overall the whole experience of bidding, winning, picking up the bike at the yard and then rebuild it in the back of my patio was amazing it was so rewarding to see that bike at the end! I just love it. I will send you a video that im still editing with whole build. Thank you copart!!

Jean Hidalgo

John Smith - Brand & Type

What was the hardest part of your truck to fix?

The hardest part was pulling the entire dash out to replace the passenger side airbag and dash skin.

How many vehicles have you rebuilt?

This was my fathers and myself first rebuild together.

What was your budget on the Rebuild?

Our budget was $13,000 we ended up coming in under budget at $10,268 and sold the truck for $18,000.

How long did it take from start to finish?

Took right around 2 months. We worked on the truck when we had free time also took sometime to find parts

Why did you pick this truck?

Was looking for something fairly easy with no major frame or cab damage for our first rebuild. Yes it was special memory to make with my father.

Did you do it all yourself?

Yes we did everything ourselves beside the paint. This was afather/son project we plan on buying more trucks from coparts to work on together.

Tell us a little about yourself.

I work as a Senior Cyber Security Specialist. My father is a retired coal miner. Was just looking for a new challenge to complete after some googling I came across the copart website and signed up.
